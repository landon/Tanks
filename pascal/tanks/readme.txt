Tankwars - PC
Landon W. Rabern, 1999

controls for blue:

tilde - moves forward
1 - fires
e - turns up
d - turns down
s - turns left
f - turns right

controls for red:

apostrophe - moves forward
enter - fires
up - turns up
down - turns down
left - turns left
right - turns right

You can edit maps in a text editor, just put down 1's for walls.
I am working on an IPX network version now.
